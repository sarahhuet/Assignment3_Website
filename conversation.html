CONVERSATION WITH A MACHINE
Maxime Black invented an AI entity and then collaborated with it to design his collection - feeding it 3,000 styling photographs of himself, and seeing how it combined them. He then physically recreated the looks according to the machine which, unable to read any fashion, cultural, or functional hierarchies in the clothes, had synthesized everything into a whole-body blob.
